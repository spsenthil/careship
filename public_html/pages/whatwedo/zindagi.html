Restored Lives through Relief and Disaster Preparedness

Relief Program Highlights

  • To grant relief and assistance to the needy victims during natural calamities such as famine, earthquake, flood, fire, pestilence, etc. and to give donations and other assistance to institutions, establishments or persons engaged in such relief work
  • To conduct awareness programs such as road safety, prevention of accidents and traffic awareness.
  • To promote solidarity and social harmony amongst cross sections of the society.
  • To carry on such other objects as may be incidental to the promotion of the aforesaid objects or any other ancillary objects relating to main objects as may be considered fit by members.
