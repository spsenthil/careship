Empowering Lives through Livelihood Programs

Livelihood Program Highlights

  • To establish, develop, maintain Vocational Training Institutes formal and Non Formal institutions, Technical training Institutes, partnering with Corporate and Private Industries
  • To promote and encourage growth of cottage and small scale industries by providing sustainable Livelihood Materials Assistance to individuals and Groups which help in economic well-being of the weaker section of the urban and rural communities.
  • To impart job oriented education, initiate job, meals for unemployed and create employment opportunities for the youth by undertaking Government and skill training Programs.
  • To promote development of Children, youth, and Women by Conducting leadership training programs, encouraging self help groups, Youth Associations, Children club forums, foster communal harmony and national integration.
  • To establish, maintain or grant aid to child labor eradication & Awareness program or projects.
  • To establish and maintain creches for the babies/children of working mothers.
